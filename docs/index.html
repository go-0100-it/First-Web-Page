Programming for the future...

This page originated from a project I was working on during one of my Nanodegrees at Udacity.
For the project, I was charged with the task of creating a web page and the topic was completely open, anything I wanted. I was unsure of exactly what I wanted but I felt I needed to create something useful, something that I could continue to build as my skills improved, and continue even after I had finished the NanoDegree. So, after a prolonged period of indecision I thought, that's it, what better subject than one that would showcase my accomplishments and skills as they improve over time.
The content would change at precisely the same time as I'd be acquiring the skills to update the design.
It would be a page constantly transforming,
evolving,
just like me.
So, that is exactly what I did and here it is,
My page.
Always complete but never finished.
